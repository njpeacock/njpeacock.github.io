About

Nick here, a Western Massachusetts native now residing in San Diego, California. This journey started back in the 90s, when me and my longtime friend,Tunde Agboola attempted to build a website from scratch using a VERY thick HTML introduction book. Unfortunately, we failed due to being young, distracted and watching WWF plus Michael Jordan dominate whenever we could, but my love for the internet was sparked from that point on.

Fast forward to 2008, my longtime friend Ryan Cowles assisted me with setting up a WordPress blog for music news and underground hip hop. I put a lot of time and effort into that blog and while it averaged less than 1k hits per month I was on a mission to understand why traffic was so low and why we only made $13.00 in adsense revenue over the entire year.

Jump to now, I have managed web and SEO campaigns for over 60 companies, my own companies, four companies have sold their businesses due to increased sales/business via my SEO strategies and I continue to learn day by day. It is my passion to work with the “underdog” and small business owners who are overshadowed by large corporations (I spent a lot of time in Corporate America and it drained my soul). If you’re seeking an experienced SEO Manager with a focus on technical SEO & strategy, I would love to hear from you.
